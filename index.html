Homemade Chicken Alfredo


E's Chicken Alfredo Recipe

Description

This meal is a personal favorite of mine, the creamy flavor, juicy sliced chicken strips and delicious pasta all compliment each other well with this dish!!


Ingredients

  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • Kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • 1 1/2c whole milk
  • 1 1/2c low-sodium chicken broth
  • Some garlic
  • 4-8oz Fetuccini
  • 1/2c heavy cream
  • 1c freshly grated Parmesan
  • Chopped parsley,for garnish

  1. Have your skillet over a med-high heat, add chicken, season with salt & pepper. Cook each side for 8-10mins Slice if you want
  2. Add the milk, broth, and garlic to the skillet, season a bit and add the fettuccine, stir for about 3min, continue cooking for 8min
  3. Stir heavy cream and Parmesan, until thick
  4. Remove from heat and stir in chicken, Garnish
