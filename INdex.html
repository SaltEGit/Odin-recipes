Lasanga


E's Lasanga

Ingredients

  • lasagna noodles
  • a teaspoon of olive oil
  • Ground Beef
  • 2-4 cloves garlic, Chopped
  • Kosher Salt
  • 16oz whole milk ricotta
  • Freshly grated Parmesan
  • A large egg
  • Sliced mozzarella

Steps

  1. Preheat oven 375 farenheit, cook lasagna noodles You can follow box directions add in a little olive oil
  2. set up a pot over med-high heat, cook the ground beef, make sure there are no pick spots Once done, place beef on skillet, adding some garlic and seasoning
  3. Add the ricotta, parmesan, parsley and egg in the mixing bowl
  4. Now, with a large casserole dish, and spread the meat sauce on the bottom of the dish, place a layer of the lassanga noodles, then a layer of the ricotta mix, mozzarella ontop of that and then repeat the layers, The last layer should be noodles with meat sauce
  5. Cover with foil, bake for 15min, then increase temp to 400, remove foil and bake for 18-20min
