Turkey Burger w/Egg

Description

A great meal to start the day, full of protein and empty on those harmful calories


Ingredients

  • Small amount of kosher salt
  • Turkey burger
  • Lettuce
  • Sliced Tomatoe
  • Burger Bun
  • An egg
  • Choice of sauce (Ketchup or BBQ)

Steps

  1. Have two pans placed on the stove, both set to med-high
  2. add a little kosher salt to both sides of the turkey burger
  3. place burger on one free pan and crack the egg and place it on the other free pan
  4. Cook burger until it is a darkened brown, and watch egg make sure each side is fried nicely
  5. You can toast the bread if you want, after you can stack your burger however you see fit.
